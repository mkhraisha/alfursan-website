# Privacy Policy

**Alfursan Auto Inc.**
Last updated: April 2026

---

## 1. Who We Are

Alfursan Auto Inc. ("Alfursan", "we", "our", or "us") is a used-vehicle dealer located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information submitted through our online financing application form at **alfursanauto.ca/financing/**.

For privacy questions, contact us at **info@alfursanauto.ca**.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

When you submit a financing application, we collect the following categories of personal information:

**Identity & Contact**
- Full legal name, date of birth, marital status
- Phone number, email address
- Home address, postal code, time at address, previous address (if less than 2 years)

**Employment & Income**
- Employment status, employer name and address, job title
- Annual gross income, length of employment
- Previous employer information (if less than 2 years at current employer)

**Vehicle & Loan Request**
- Vehicle of interest (year, make, model, price)
- Requested down payment and loan term
- Vehicle Identification Number (VIN) if available

**Identity Document (optional)**
- Images of the front and back of your driver's licence, uploaded only if you choose to include them

We do **not** collect Social Insurance Numbers (SINs).

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

- Evaluate your financing application and connect you with lending partners
- Contact you about your application by phone or email
- Maintain records required by applicable law
- Improve our services and prevent fraud

We do **not** sell your personal information to third parties.

---

## 4. Who We Share Your Information With

| Recipient | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| Lending partners & financial institutions | Processing your financing application |
| Supabase Inc. (database & file storage) | Secure storage of application data; servers located in AWS us-east-1 (USA) |
| Resend Inc. (transactional email) | Sending confirmation and notification emails |

All third-party processors are contractually bound to use your information only as instructed by us.

Because some data is processed in the United States, it may be subject to access by U.S. authorities under applicable law. We take reasonable steps to ensure your information receives equivalent protection.

---

## 5. Data Retention

We retain your application data for **7 years** from the date of submission, consistent with Ontario's consumer protection record-keeping requirements, after which it is securely deleted.

Driver's licence images are stored in a private, encrypted storage bucket. Images uploaded but not associated with a completed application are automatically deleted within **24 hours**.

---

## 6. Security

We use industry-standard safeguards including:

- TLS encryption for all data in transit
- Encrypted storage at rest (Supabase/AWS)
- Access controls limiting dashboard access to authorised Alfursan staff only
- Audit logs recording every staff access to your application

---

## 7. Your Rights (PIPEDA & Ontario)

Under Canada's *Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act* (PIPEDA) and Ontario's *Consumer Protection Act*, you have the right to:

- **Access** the personal information we hold about you
- **Correct** inaccurate information
- **Withdraw consent** (subject to legal or contractual restrictions)
- **Request deletion** of your information, where no legal retention obligation applies

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at **info@alfursanauto.ca** with your name and the reference number from your application confirmation email. We will respond within **30 days**.

---

## 8. Cookies

The financing application form uses **sessionStorage** (browser-only, not a cookie) to save your draft locally while you complete the form. No tracking cookies are set on the financing pages.

---

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of our financing form after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
